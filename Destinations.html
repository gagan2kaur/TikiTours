Destinations

1. Marokopa Falls

At 35m high, the Marokopa falls are said to be one of the most beautiful in the country and are well worth a visit.Here the Marokopa River cascades over the undercut greywacke basement rock. Take the short track through tawa and nikau forest down to the viewing platform.

Ref:"https://www.doc.govt.nz/parks-and-recreation/places-to-go/waikato/places/waitomo-area/tracks/marokopa-falls-walk/"

2. Ruakuri Cave

Ruakuri Cave is an experience that must be seen to be believed.Begin the tour by journeying down through the spectacular, man-made spiral entrance, marvel at the softly folding shawl-like limestone formations and crystal tapestries while listening to the distant thunder of subterranean waterfalls.

Ref:https://www.waitomo.com/glowworms-and-caves/ruakuri-cave

3.Te Kuiti

Te Kuiti is a good base for exploring the Waitomo Caves area or cycling the Timber Trail.The prime time to visit Te Kuiti is in autumn when Te Kuiti puts on the Great NZ Muster, normally held the weekend after Easter each year.

Ref:"https://www.newzealand.com/nz/te-kuiti/"
